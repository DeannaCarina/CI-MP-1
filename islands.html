The Lucayan Archipelago (also known as the Bahama Archipelago), is an island group comprising the Commonwealth of The Bahamas and the British Overseas Territory of the Turks and Caicos Islands. The archipelago is in the western North Atlantic Ocean, north of Cuba and the other Antilles, and east and southeast of Florida. The Lucayan Archipelago is not located in the Caribbean Sea.

Cuba

Back to the top

Click OR tap the map to see it bigger!

Cuba, (officially the Republic of Cuba), is a country comprising the island of Cuba, as well as Isla de la Juventud and several minor archipelagos. Cuba is located where the northern Caribbean Sea, Gulf of Mexico, and Atlantic Ocean meet. It is east of the Yucatán Peninsula (Mexico), south of both the U.S. state of Florida and the Bahamas, west of Hispaniola, and north of both Jamaica and the Cayman Islands. Havana is the largest city and capital; other major cities include Santiago de Cuba and Camagüey.

Cayman Islands and Jamaica

Back to the top

Click OR tap the map to see it bigger!

The Cayman Islands is a British Overseas Territory in the western Caribbean Sea. The 264-square-kilometre territory comprises the three islands of Grand Cayman, Cayman Brac and Little Cayman. The capital city is George Town on Grand Cayman, which is the most populous of the three islands. Jamaica is an island country situated in the Caribbean Sea. Spanning 10,990 square kilometres in area, it is the third-largest island of the Greater Antilles and the Caribbean.

Hispaniola and Puerto Rico

Back to the top

Click OR tap the map to see it bigger!

Hispaniola is an island in the Caribbean that is part of the Greater Antilles, it is the most populous island in the West Indies, and the region's second largest in area, after the main island of Cuba. The 76,192-square-kilometre island is divided into two separate nations: the Spanish-speaking Dominican Republic to the east and French/Haitian Creole-speaking Haiti to the west. Puerto Rico (officially the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico) is a Caribbean island and unincorporated territory of the United States. It is located in the northeast Caribbean Sea, approximately 1,000 miles southeast of Miami, Florida.

Leeward Islands

Back to the top

Click OR tap the map to see it bigger!

The Leeward Islands are a group of islands situated where the northeastern Caribbean Sea meets the western Atlantic Ocean. Starting with the Virgin Islands east of Puerto Rico, they extend southeast to Guadeloupe and its dependencies. The regions of the Leeward islands are:
  • The Virgin Islands:
    • British Vigin Islands, the Spanish Vigin Islands and the US Vigin Islands.
  • Northern Leeward Islands (Antiuga and above):
    • Anguilla, Saint Martin, Antigua & Barbuda, Saint Barthélemy and Saba
  • Southern Leeward Islands (Sint Eustatius and below):
    • Sint Eustatius, Saint Kitts and Nevis, Montserrat and Guadeloupe
Saint Barthélemy & Guadeloupe & St Martin (French Side):
  • Official Language: French
  • Official Currency: Euros
Saba, Sint Eustatius & St Martin (Dutch Side):
  • Official Language: Dutch
  • Official Currency: United States Dollar
Anguilla, Saint Kitts and Nevis, Antigua and Barbuda & Montserrat:
  • Official Language: English
  • Official Currency: Eastern Caribbean Dollar

Windward Islands

Back to the top

Click OR tap the map to see it bigger!

The Windward Islands, are the southern, generally larger islands of the Lesser Antilles, within the West Indies. They lie south of the Leeward Islands. The name was also used to refer to a British colony in these islands, existing between 1833 and 1960 and consisting of the islands of SAINT LUCIA, SAINT VINCENT & THE GRENADINES, and GRENADA, which today make up three sovereign nations. The island of DOMINICA, also today a sovereign nation, was traditionally a part of the Leeward Islands until 1940, when it was transferred from the Leeward Islands colony to the Windward Islands. MARTINIQUE is also situated in the Windward island chain, and is a single territorial collectivity of France.

Saint Lucia, Saint Vincent & The Grenadines, Grenada and Dominica:
  • Official Language: English
  • Official Currency: Eastern Caribbean Dollar*
Martinique
  • Official Language: French
  • Official Currency: Euro

*The US Dollar is also widely used on these islands in tourist areas, however purchase change may be given in ECD.

Trinidad & Tobago and Barbados

Back to the top

Click OR tap the map to see it bigger!

TRINIDAD & TOBAGO, is the southernmost island country in the Caribbean and is known for its fossil-fuel wealth. Consisting of the main islands Trinidad and Tobago, and numerous much smaller islands, it is situated 130 kilometres south of Grenada and 11 kilometres off the coast of northeastern Venezuela. BARBADOS is an island country in the Lesser Antilles of the West Indies, in the Caribbean region of North America, and the most easterly of the Caribbean Islands. It covers an area of 432 km2. It is in the western part of the North Atlantic, 100 km east of the Windward Islands and the Caribbean Sea. Barbados is outside the principal Atlantic hurricane belt. Its capital and largest city is Bridgetown.

Trinidad & Tobago:
  • Official Language: English
  • Official Currency: Trinidad & Tobago Dollar
Barbados:
  • Official Language: English
  • Official Currency: Barbados Dollar

ABC Islands

Back to the top

Click OR tap the map to see it bigger!

The ABC islands is the physical group of ARUBA, BONAIRE, and CURAÇO, the three westernmost islands of the Leeward Antilles in the Caribbean Sea. They are a short distance north of Falcón State, Venezuela. Aruba and Curaçao are autonomous, self-governing constituent countries of the Netherlands, while Bonaire is a special municipality of the Netherlands. Territories of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, the countries, and municipality, are outside the European Union.

Aruba:
  • Official Language: Dutch & Papiamento
  • Official Currency: Aruban Florin (USD widely accepted)
Bonaire:
  • Official Language: Dutch
  • Official Currency: US Dollar
Curaçao:
  • Official Language: Dutch, English & Papiamento
  • Official Currency: Netherlands Antillean Guilder (USD widely accepted)
